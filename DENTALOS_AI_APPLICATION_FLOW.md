# DentalOS AI — Complete Application Flow Documentation

> Master product documentation for the DentalOS AI demo application. Grounded in the actual implementation (`src/types`, `src/mock`, `src/stores`, `src/app/(app)/*`) as of this writing. Tech stack: Next.js 16 (React 19), Zustand 5 (persisted), Tailwind CSS 4, shadcn/ui (Radix), Recharts, FullCalendar, Framer Motion, next-themes.

---

## 1. Platform Overview

**What is DentalOS AI**
DentalOS AI is a dental practice operating system that combines a traditional practice-management UI (patients, appointments, clinical, claims, billing) with a simulated layer of autonomous "AI agents" that perform front-desk, clinical documentation, insurance, billing, and recall work. The demo is a fully client-side Next.js app — all data lives in seeded JSON mock files and Zustand stores persisted to `localStorage`; there is no real backend, and AI responses (CEO Copilot, call transcripts, SOAP notes) are pre-scripted rather than generated by a live LLM.

**Core vision**
Show a single-pane-of-glass dental practice platform where an "AI Workforce" of 8 named agents handles repetitive operational work (booking, verification, claims, recalls, billing, documentation) under human oversight, while a CEO Copilot gives practice owners conversational access to business insights, and a Workflow Engine lets staff automate event-driven processes without code.

**Primary modules**
Dashboard, Patients, Appointments, Clinical, Imaging, Insurance, Claims, Billing, Communications, AI Workforce, Workflows, Analytics, CEO Copilot, Integrations, Staff & RBAC, Settings.

**Target users**
Multi-clinic dental service organizations (DSOs) and independent practices. Personas modeled directly in the seed data: Super Admin, DSO Admin, Clinic Owner, Dentist, Front Desk, Insurance Coordinator, Billing Manager.

## 3. Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

Permissions are evaluated per module at three levels — **Full** (view+edit), **Read** (view only), **None** (hidden from sidebar and gated by `PermissionGuard`). Super Admin, DSO Admin, and Clinic Owner are hard-coded to **Full** everywhere in `usePermission()`.

| Role | Patients | Appointments | Clinical | Claims | Billing | Analytics | Settings | AI Agents |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Super Admin** | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full |
| **DSO Admin** | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full |
| **Clinic Owner** | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full |
| **Dentist** | Full | Full | Full | Read | Read | Read | None | None |
| **Front Desk** | Read | Full | None | Read | None | None | None | None |
| **Insurance Coordinator** | Read | Read | None | Full | Read | Read | None | None |
| **Billing Manager** | Read | None | None | Full | Full | Full | None | None |

**MFA enforcement:** high-privilege roles (Super Admin, DSO Admin, Clinic Owner, Dentist) require a 6-digit MFA code at login (`mfaEnabled: true`, fixed demo code per user); Front Desk, Insurance Coordinator and Billing Manager bypass MFA in this configuration.

**Enforcement mechanism:**
- `usePermission(module)` — resolves the level for the current route/module.
- `useCanEdit(module)` — `true` only when level is `Full`; gates buttons (New Appointment, Add Patient, Submit Claim, etc.).
- `PermissionGuard` component — renders a full "Access Restricted" screen with a lock icon for `None`, or a blue "read-only" banner for `Read`.

**Per-role journeys:**
- **Super Admin / DSO Admin** — cross-org oversight; only roles that see every clinic's data via `activeOrgId` switching and full Staff/Audit Logs access.
- **Clinic Owner** — single-clinic full control; primary user of CEO Copilot and Analytics.
- **Dentist** — clinical workflow: Patients → Appointments → Clinical (SOAP) → read-only visibility into claims/billing/analytics for their own production.
- **Front Desk** — scheduling and check-in focused; can edit Appointments, read-only on Patients, no access to Clinical, Billing, Settings, or AI Agents.
- **Insurance Coordinator** — owns Claims end-to-end (Full), read access to Patients/Appointments/Billing/Analytics for context, no Clinical access.
- **Billing Manager** — owns Claims and Billing (Full), Analytics Full for revenue reporting, no Appointments or Clinical access.

## 4. Module-by-Module Breakdown

### Dashboard (`/dashboard`)
- **Purpose:** Daily operational command center.
- **KPI Cards:** Total Revenue (MoM trend), Active Patients, Claims Pending, AI Automation Rate.
- **Charts:** Revenue over 7D/30D/12M (Recharts).
- **AI involvement:** Automation Rate KPI is computed from agent task data.
- **Connected workflows:** Links into Patients, Claims, AI Workforce.

### Patients (`/patients`, `/patients/[id]`)
- **Purpose:** Clinical/demographic CRM and ledger.
- **Features:** Searchable/filterable list with quick actions; detail view with demographics, primary/secondary insurance (PPO/HMO, deductible, annual max, coverage %), medical history, allergies, medications, dental history, treatment plans, claims, invoices, communications log, documents, and a **Journey** timeline (stage, status, attributed AI agent).
- **Actions:** Add New Patient, edit record, view ledger.
- **AI involvement:** Journey steps can be attributed to an AI agent (e.g., AI Receptionist booked the visit).
- **Data displayed:** Risk score, lifetime value, outstanding balance, recall-due date, next appointment.

### Appointments (`/appointments`)
- **Purpose:** Daily/weekly scheduling and check-in.
- **Features:** FullCalendar-based day/week view; simulated incoming-call ringtone via the Web Audio API; New Appointment action.
- **Data displayed:** Chair, dentist, type, status (Scheduled/Confirmed/In Progress/Completed/Cancelled/No Show), insurance-verified flag, pre-auth-required flag, estimated revenue.

### Clinical (`/clinical`)
- **Purpose:** Charting visits and SOAP documentation.
- **KPI Cards:** Daily Patients, Notes Completed, AI Time Saved, Missing Notes.
- **Features:** New Note dialog (patient, 12 visit types, tooth number); **AI SOAP Generator** auto-drafts Subjective/Objective/Assessment/Plan based on visit type; session-local "Notes Added This Session" expandable list.
- **AI involvement:** AI Scribe agent drafts SOAP content.

### Imaging (`/imaging`)
- **Purpose:** X-ray/CBCT/intraoral photo review.
- **Features:** Image grid with mock AI caries-detection overlay highlighting potential cavities.
- **AI involvement:** AI Radiology (simulated detection markers, not a live agent in `agentStore`).

### Insurance (`/insurance`)
- **Purpose:** Eligibility verification and pre-authorization tracking.
- **KPI Cards:** Clean Claim Rate, Avg Days to Pay, Total Denied.
- **Features:** Verification status badges, coverage breakdown (preventive/basic/major/ortho %).
- **AI involvement:** AI Insurance agent performs verification/pre-auth tasks logged to the agent's task log.

### Claims (`/claims`, `/claims/[id]`)
- **Purpose:** Insurance claim lifecycle management.
- **Data model:** CDT procedure codes (code, description, tooth, fee), totals billed/allowed/paid, patient responsibility, status (Draft→Submitted→Pending→Approved/Denied/Appealed→Paid), denial reason, **aiScore** (0–100) and **aiFlags** for risk assessment, attachments.
- **AI involvement:** AI Claims agent prepares, submits, and appeals claims; risk scoring surfaces likely denials before submission.

### Billing (`/billing`)
- **Purpose:** Patient statements and accounts receivable.
- **KPI Cards:** Total Collections, AR > 90 Days, Patient Balances.
- **Features:** Payment collection dialog, invoice generation, payment history (Cash/Check/Credit Card/ACH/CareCredit).
- **AI involvement:** AI Billing agent manages AR follow-ups and statement delivery.

### Communications (`/communications`)
- **Purpose:** Patient outreach across SMS, Email, Voice.
- **KPI Cards:** Total Messages, Active Campaigns, Avg Review Rating, Total Recipients.
- **Features:** Two-way Inbox; New Campaign wizard (Recall, Reactivation, Birthday, Review — with Email/SMS/Voice script steps); Send Review Request dialog (Google/Yelp links); reusable templates.
- **AI involvement:** AI Recall drives recall/reactivation campaigns; messages can be flagged `agentGenerated`.

### AI Workforce (`/ai-workforce`, `/ai-workforce/[agent]`)
- **Purpose:** Monitor and configure the 8 autonomous agents.
- **List page:** 4-column agent grid (avatar, status pulse, tasks/success%/revenue, 7-day sparkline, current activity), live activity feed strip refreshing every 6s, Configure sheet (confidence threshold slider 50–99%, auto-approve toggle, notifications toggle, working-hours selector).
- **Detail page:** stat cards (tasks/success/revenue), task log, configuration panel (status toggle, working hours, confidence/auto-approve thresholds), 90-day performance line chart. The Receptionist detail additionally includes a **Call Recordings** section with a Call Player modal (Web Speech API TTS playback, transcript highlighting, extracted-data callouts, downloadable transcript).
- **Connected workflows:** Agent activities populate notifications, patient journey steps, and dashboard automation-rate KPI.

### Workflows (`/workflows`)
- **Purpose:** No-code, event-driven automation.
- **Features:** Summary cards (Total Actions, Active/Paused/Draft counts); 2-column workflow cards (trigger, step count, action count, last run, Play/Pause/Edit/Duplicate/Delete); Create Workflow dialog (name, trigger dropdown of 8 events, description). New workflows start as Draft, 1 step, 0 actions.
- **Note:** trigger-based, no visual drag-and-drop builder in the current implementation — editing happens at the workflow-card level.

### Analytics (`/analytics`)
- **Purpose:** Owner-level financial/operational reporting.
- **Features:** Production vs. Collections charts filterable by 7D/30D/12M.

### CEO Copilot (`/ceo-copilot`)
- **Purpose:** Conversational executive Q&A over practice data.
- **Layout:** Left sidebar with prior conversations + New Conversation; right pane chat thread with streaming (50ms/word) assistant responses, typing indicator, 6 suggested questions (revenue drop, claims at risk, overdue recalls, revenue opportunities, AI workforce performance, etc.).
- **AI involvement:** Responses are pre-scripted markdown (tables, bold, bullet recommendations) referencing real KPIs and deep-linking to `/claims`, `/communications`, etc. — not a live LLM call in this build.

### Integrations (`/integrations`, `/integrations/[id]`)
- **Purpose:** Third-party connection management.
- **List page:** summary cards (Connected count, Avg Health Score, Total Records Synced), grid of integration cards with health bar and sync frequency.
- **Detail page:** connection toggle, field-mapping config, sync history, API usage chart, error logs with resolved/unresolved state, Test Connection action.

### Staff & RBAC (`/staff`)
- **Purpose:** HR, permission management, security auditing.
- **Features:** User management table; Role Matrix tab (visual permission grid); Audit Logs tab (Time, User, Action, Module, IP, Result with color-coded Success/Denied badges).

### Settings (`/settings`)
- **Purpose:** Practice configuration.
- **Features:** Clinic details, API/integration key management, system preferences (theme, notifications).

## 5. Patient Journey Flow

**Lead → Appointment → Insurance → Visit → Imaging → Treatment → Claims → Billing → Recall → Revenue Insights**

| Stage | User actions | AI actions | System actions | Status transition |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Lead | Staff or patient initiates contact | AI Receptionist answers/books call | Patient record created (`PAT-xxxx`) | — → New Patient |
| Appointment | Front Desk confirms/reschedules | AI Receptionist books/reminds | Appointment status set | Scheduled → Confirmed |
| Insurance | Insurance Coordinator reviews | AI Insurance verifies eligibility/pre-auth | `eligibilityVerified` flag set | Unverified → Verified |
| Visit | Dentist checks in patient | — | Appointment status updates | Confirmed → In Progress → Completed |
| Imaging | Dentist reviews images | AI Radiology highlights caries risk | Images attached to chart | — |
| Treatment | Dentist documents visit, proposes plan | AI Scribe drafts SOAP note | Treatment plan created | Proposed → Accepted/Declined → In Progress → Completed |
| Claims | Insurance Coordinator reviews/submits | AI Claims preps, scores, submits, appeals | Claim status updates, `aiScore`/`aiFlags` set | Draft → Submitted → Pending → Approved/Denied/Appealed → Paid |
| Billing | Billing Manager collects/follows up | AI Billing sends statements/AR follow-ups | Invoice/payment recorded | Outstanding → Partial → Paid (or Overdue) |
| Recall | — | AI Recall triggers reminder campaigns | `recallDue` evaluated, workflow fires | Patient flagged due → contacted → rebooked |
| Revenue Insights | Clinic Owner asks CEO Copilot | AI Revenue surfaces unscheduled-treatment/upsell opportunities | Journey step logged with agent attribution | Insight surfaced → action taken |

Every stage transition can be written into the patient's `journey: JourneyStep[]`, recording stage, date, status, and the agent (if any) responsible — giving a full audit trail of human vs. AI actions.

## 6. AI Workforce Documentation

| Agent | Purpose | Trigger | Inputs | Outputs | Screens | Business value |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **AI Receptionist** | Phone-based booking & inquiries | Inbound/outbound call event | Caller phone, intent | Appointment booked, patient record, call transcript | `/ai-workforce/agent-receptionist`, Appointments | Captures after-hours/overflow demand without added headcount |
| **AI Scribe** | Clinical documentation | New clinical note started | Visit type, tooth number | Draft SOAP note (S/O/A/P) | Clinical, `/ai-workforce/agent-scribe` | Cuts documentation time per visit |
| **AI Insurance** | Eligibility & pre-auth | New appointment / plan created | Insurance policy data | Verified eligibility, pre-auth status | Insurance, `/ai-workforce/agent-insurance` | Reduces day-of-visit coverage surprises |
| **AI Claims** | Claim prep, submission, appeals | Treatment completed / claim denied | CDT codes, treatment plan | Submitted claim, `aiScore`, `aiFlags`, appeal | Claims, `/ai-workforce/agent-claims` | Improves clean-claim rate, recovers denials |
| **AI Recall** | Recall/reactivation outreach | `recallDue` date approaching | Patient recall date, contact prefs | SMS/Email/Voice campaign sends | Communications, `/ai-workforce/agent-recall` | Fills schedule gaps, recovers lapsed patients |
| **AI Billing** | AR management & statements | Invoice overdue threshold | Invoice balance, due date | Statement sent, follow-up logged | Billing, `/ai-workforce/agent-billing` | Shrinks AR > 90 days |
| **AI Revenue** | Opportunity identification | Periodic scan of unscheduled treatment | Treatment plans, patient LTV | Flagged revenue opportunities | Analytics, `/ai-workforce/agent-revenue` | Surfaces unrealized production |
| **CEO Copilot** | Executive insight Q&A | User question in chat | Aggregated KPIs across all modules | Narrative answer + deep links | `/ceo-copilot` | Gives owners instant, conversational BI |

All agents share: status (`active`/`idle`/`paused`), `tasksToday`, `successRate`, `revenueToday`, a 7-day sparkline, and a per-agent configurable confidence threshold + auto-approve threshold + working-hours window. Activity is simulated client-side (`agentStore.getNextActivity()`, refreshing ~every 6s) rather than driven by a live LLM backend.

## 9. Live Agent Monitoring

- **Agent status tracking:** each `AIAgent` carries `status` (active/idle/paused), toggled via `agentStore.toggleAgent(id)` from the workforce grid or detail page.
- **Activity feed:** `AgentActivityFeed` component renders the most recent `AgentActivity` entries (action, outcome, related patient/claim, timestamp), animated in/out with Framer Motion; the workforce list page keeps the 15 most recent activities, refreshing roughly every 6–8 seconds via `getNextActivity()`.
- **Task execution:** simulated client-side — no real task queue or backend execution; activities are generated from a template pool and prepended to the activity array (capped at 50).
- **Performance metrics:** `tasksToday`, `successRate`, `revenueToday`, 7-day sparkline on the grid; 90-day Tasks vs. Success-Rate line chart on the agent detail page.
- **Real-time simulations:** Receptionist call recordings are played back via the Web Speech API with distinct voice/pitch for "AI" vs. "Patient" lines, a progress bar, and a fabricated waveform — illustrating realism without a live telephony backend.

## 11. Demo Mode Flow

`uiStore` exposes `activeDemo: boolean` and `demoStep: number` with `setActiveDemo()`/`setDemoStep()` actions, indicating a guided walkthrough capability. Based on the seeded data and module order, the intended guided flow is:

1. **Login** — pick a seeded user (e.g., Sarah Martinez, Clinic Owner) → MFA screen shows the access-preview permission matrix before code entry.
2. **Dashboard** — land on KPI overview; revenue/automation cards animate in.
3. **Patients** — open a high-LTV patient (e.g., Marcus Jackson `PAT-0099`) to show full chart, insurance, journey timeline.
4. **Appointments** — view the day's schedule; a simulated inbound call triggers the ringtone.
5. **AI Workforce** — open AI Receptionist agent detail, play a call recording end-to-end (transcript highlight + extracted-data callouts).
6. **Clinical** — start a new note, trigger the AI SOAP Generator for an Exam visit type.
7. **Imaging** — open an X-ray showing the AI caries-detection overlay.
8. **Insurance / Claims** — show a claim with a high `aiScore` flag and walk through Submitted → Approved.
9. **Billing** — collect a payment via the payment dialog; show AR > 90 days.
10. **Communications** — launch a Recall campaign wizard; show the AI-generated SMS preview.
11. **Workflows** — show the Annual Recall Reminder workflow firing (892 actions taken).
12. **Analytics** — switch the Production vs. Collections chart between 7D/30D/12M.
13. **CEO Copilot** — ask "Why did revenue drop this month?" and watch the streamed, markdown-formatted answer with deep links.
14. **Integrations** — show DentalXChange/Stripe health scores and a recent sync.
15. **Staff** — show the Role Matrix and an Audit Log entry for a denied access attempt.

Every step above maps to an existing screen/component; no step requires data outside what's already seeded.

## 15. End-to-End User Stories

**New patient booking**
A prospective patient calls the practice after hours. AI Receptionist answers, captures demographics, checks chair/dentist availability, and books a Cleaning appointment. A new `Patient` record (`PAT-xxxx`) is created with `journey` stage "Lead → Appointment" attributed to `agent-receptionist`. Front Desk sees the new appointment on `/appointments` the next morning and confirms it; a notification ("New patient added") appears in the bell menu.

**Emergency patient**
A patient calls in pain. AI Receptionist classifies it as urgent, books an Emergency-type appointment same-day, and flags the chair. Front Desk sees the booking highlighted in `/appointments`; the dentist opens Clinical immediately after the visit and uses the AI SOAP Generator to draft notes for the Emergency visit type, then creates a treatment plan.

**Insurance verification**
Insurance Coordinator opens `/insurance` for a newly booked patient. AI Insurance has already run eligibility verification against the patient's primary policy, setting `eligibilityVerified: true` and populating deductible/annual-max/coverage percentages. The Coordinator reviews and confirms before the visit, avoiding a day-of-surprise.

**Claim submission**
After a completed Filling visit, AI Claims assembles the CDT codes into a claim, runs a risk score, and finds an `aiFlag` for a missing X-ray attachment. It pauses for human review; the Insurance Coordinator attaches the image from `/imaging`. The claim moves Draft → Submitted, then later updates to Approved or Denied — if Denied, the **Claim Denial Appeal** workflow triggers AI Claims to draft an appeal.

**Recall campaign**
The **Annual Recall Reminder** workflow fires for patients whose `recallDue` is 30 days out. AI Recall sends a personalized SMS/Email via the Communications module; if the patient doesn't respond in 7 days, escalates to a phone reminder logged as a `CommunicationEntry` with `agentGenerated: true`. The Clinic Owner sees recall campaign performance on the AI Workforce agent-recall detail page (124 tasks, 76% success).

**Revenue optimization**
AI Revenue scans treatment plans and patient history, flags unscheduled high-value procedures and lapsed-recall patients with high lifetime value. The Clinic Owner asks CEO Copilot "Where are our revenue opportunities?" and receives a markdown-formatted answer naming specific patients (e.g., David Kim) and dollar amounts, with a deep link into `/patients/[id]` to act on it directly.

**CEO asking business questions**
The Clinic Owner opens `/ceo-copilot`, selects the suggested question "Why did revenue drop this month?". The assistant streams a structured analysis (key numbers, likely causes such as a spike in Delta Dental denials, recommended actions) referencing live KPI data from Claims and Analytics, with inline links back to `/claims` and `/communications` so the owner can act immediately without leaving the conversation.
